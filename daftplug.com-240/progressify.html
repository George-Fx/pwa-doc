Any Questions?

Answers to the most frequently asked questions about Progressify.

What are Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)?

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) are web apps built and enhanced with modern APIs to provide enhanced capabilities while still reaching any web user on any device with a single codebase. They combine the broad reach of web apps with the rich capabilities of platform-specific apps to enhance the user experience.

What is the benefit of having a PWA over a native app?

PWAs are more cost-effective to develop and maintain than native apps since there's no need to create separate Android and iOS apps or manage them on app stores. Your website can be installed directly from the browser like an app, without taking up space on users' devices.

Can I have both PWA and iOS and Android apps?

Yes! That's the beauty of having a PWA. With Progressify, you can enhance your website with PWA features for a native app-like experience in browsers and also generate real iOS and Android apps for app store publication, reaching billions of users. iOS and Android apps generated by Progressify don't require updates, as they reflect your website in real time—any changes you make will automatically appear in the apps.

Is Progressify compatible with my theme?

Progressify is compatible with any WordPress theme, plugin or any other WordPress configuration, be it standard WordPress website, e-commerce, membership, affiliate, social network, etc. It is a standalone plugin that does not require or depend on anything else to function.

Which browsers are supported by Progressify?

Most of the PWA functionality is supported by all major browsers, including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, and Microsoft Edge. Additionally, Progressify is designed to be compatible with all the browsers by adapting PWA features it provides to the browser's capabilities.

Which devices are supported by Progressify?

Progressify is compatible with all devices that support the browsers it is designed for. This includes desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. Additionally, the PWA functionality is designed to be compatible with all devices, so it will work on any device that can run the browser it is installed on.
